About Us

We are a mental health campaign looking to spread awareness about high-functioning anxiety! Everyday students just like us suffer from perfectionism and high expectations that lead to anxiety over their value as a person; our goal is to spread awareness for that very issue and teach others that their mind is a garden and needs to be nurtured every day!
